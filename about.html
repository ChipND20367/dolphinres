About Dolphin Res

Safe, affordable and community-driven student accommodation, built around what students actually need to succeed.

Our Story

Our Story

Dolphin Res exists for one simple reason: students deserve a place to live that feels safe, feels affordable, and actually supports their studies. We saw too many students juggling long commutes, unreliable utilities and accommodation that didn't put their wellbeing first — so we set out to do it differently.

Every part of Dolphin Res, from our 24/7 security to our study-friendly common spaces, is designed around the everyday reality of student life near UKZN Westville.

View Our Residence

Our Mission

We believe accommodation shouldn't be another obstacle between a student and their degree. That's why we keep our residence NSFAS accredited, fee-transparent, and free of hidden costs — so students can focus on what matters.

Beyond four walls and a bed, we're building a community: a place where students feel supported, connected, and genuinely at home while they study.
